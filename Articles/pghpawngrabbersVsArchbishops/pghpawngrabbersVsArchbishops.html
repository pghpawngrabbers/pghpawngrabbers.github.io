Pawngrabbers vs. Archbishops

Recap of Pawngrabbers vs. Archbishops, Feb 26, 2019

The Pittsburgh Pawngrabbers had a close match this week against the St. Louis Archbishops including many exciting games as these two teams went all out for the win. Things started out very promising for the Pawngrabbers as the team found itself up 3 - 1 in the first round. As the match progressed, the tension only grew, culminating at the end of the third round where the teams were tied with 6 points each. 

The team had this to say:

Final round's in. The @pghpawngrabbers falls short against @stlarchbishops 7.5:8.5. #prochess

— Pittsburgh Pawngrabbers (@pghpawngrabbers) February 27, 2019

The Pawngrabbers put up a good fight against one of the Atlantic division's best teams with some great performances from the top 3 boards. Considering the stiff competition posed by the enemy team, a score this close is promising for the Pawngrabbers


Match Highlights


This match had several stellar games, but the highlight among all of them is IM Tuan Minh Le's immaculate victory against GM Wesley So. The game below is a testament to how much effort and dedication he's put into these PRO Chess League matches.

Le vs. So

While the scores didn't end up in his team's favor, IM Minh Le definitely walked away from this match with something to be proud of. IM Minh Le has consistently given excellent performances in the matches that he's played, but defeating the United States' second best player is a whole other feat entirely.


Next Week: Webster Windmills


On Wednesday, March 6th, The Pawngrabbers go head to head with the Webster Windmills. While a playoff spot will be a difficult goal, scoring well against the Windmills will help to cement the Pawngrabbers' current standing. The Windmills are currently the third best team in the Atlantic Division, and if the Pawngrabbers' score against the Archbishops (the division's best team) is any indication, next week's match looks exciting from the get-go.
