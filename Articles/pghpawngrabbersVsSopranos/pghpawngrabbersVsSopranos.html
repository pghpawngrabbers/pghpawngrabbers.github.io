Pawngrabbers vs. Sopranos

Recap of Pawngrabbers vs. Sopranos, Jan 22, 2019

An unfortunate result in yesterday's matchup between the Pittsburgh Pawngrabbers and the Montclair Sopranos as the Pawngrabbers fell to their heated rivals. The loss brings the Pawngrabbers record to 0-3 in the season and they sit bottom in their division. 

The team had this to say about the result: 

As the match concludes we have an unfortunate loss against @MntclrSopranos, but well played from our players here at the pawngrabbers you have a lot to be proud of. pic.twitter.com/LsbZVK3P2q

— Pittsburgh Pawngrabbers (@pghpawngrabbers) January 23, 2019
While the recent results may seem grim, it is important to keep in mind that it is still very much early into the season and there is time for the Pawngrabbers to turn it around. Furthermore, the losses against Montreal and New York seem less upsetting when their standings are factored in. Montreal sits at the top of the division and New York is in second place. As the season continues, the newly acquired Pawngrabber players will also have an opportunity to get acclimated to the new team which will likely boost their performance.

Highlights From the Game

While the scoreline may show that the Sopranos were in cruise control throughout much of the game, the Pawngrabbers did show flashes of brilliance. The most noticeable was GM Shabalov's game of the week contender win against GM Gadir Guseinov. 

GM Evgeny Postny, recently signed from the Sopranos, also had a good showing winning two out of his four games and drawing a third. He definitely had something to prove against his former team. These highlights show that while the results may have not favored the Pawngrabbers, the team has formidable strength that it will demonstrate as the season continues. 

Next Week: Battle Royale

Next week brings up the first battle royale matchup of the season. The battle royale game mode consists of a team playing one single four-game round against seven separate teams. The battle royale brings many interesting dynamics into play. The Pawngrabbers will have the opportunity to play teams outside their division and see how they matchup. It will be a crucial learning ground for the team to further assess their own weaknesses as well as looking at the weaknesses of other teams. The assessments will be crucial if the Pawngrabbers make it into the playoffs. While the Pawngrabbers may currently be 0-3, they have a formidable lineup and future looks exciting. The next few games of the season will show what they can do.
